Meet the cast!

Meet Dougie!
Dougie is our resident wise-guy, and the younger brother of Pablo. A great lover of bad puns and interesting facts, people often say their first impression of Dougie was that of a cold and logical smartie. But Dougie's friends will be glad to tell you that this smart cookie has a warm and gooey center- in fact Dougie is a very kind and gentle creature! He's always glad to make a new friend, and only about 70% of that motive is so he can tell all of his interesting facts and re-used jokes to fresh ears.
Dougie is so glad to meet you!
